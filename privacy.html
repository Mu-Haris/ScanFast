Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 23, 2026

ScanFast ("we", "us", "the app") is built and operated by Kinex Apps. This policy explains exactly what data the app handles, what stays on your device, and what is shared with the limited third parties we use to keep the app free.

Summary

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your scanned documents. Every scan, edit, and PDF stays on your iPhone.
  • We do not require an account. There is no sign-up, no login, no cloud sync.
  • We show ads via Google AdMob to keep the app free. AdMob collects standard advertising identifiers in line with the consent you provide on first launch.
  • You are in control. You can revoke ad personalization at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Information we collect

Information ScanFast itself collects

None. ScanFast does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information, scanned images, or PDFs on any server. All scanning, enhancement, page editing, signatures, and PDF generation happen entirely on your device. Your documents are saved only to local on-device storage managed by iOS.

Information collected by third-party services

To keep the app free, ScanFast uses the following third-party SDKs that may collect limited information on our behalf:

  • Google AdMob — serves banner, interstitial, and native advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (including the Identifier for Advertisers / IDFA when you opt in via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt), IP address, coarse location derived from IP, ad interaction events, and basic device information (model, OS version). See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) — handles GDPR / IDFA consent prompts on first launch. UMP stores your consent choice locally and shares it with AdMob.

iOS permissions

  • Camera — used only when you tap the scan button, so the app can capture pages. Images are written to local app storage and never uploaded.
  • App Tracking (ATT) — you will see Apple's tracking prompt on first launch. Tapping "Ask App Not to Track" disables IDFA-based ad personalization. Ads still appear but will be non-personalized.

Required-reason API declarations

In accordance with Apple's privacy requirements, ScanFast declares its use of the following required-reason APIs in its privacy manifest:

  • UserDefaults — to store your in-app preferences (layout, autocapture toggle, onboarding completion flag, consent state).
  • File timestamp APIs — to display and sort your scanned documents by date.

How long we retain your data

Because ScanFast does not collect your documents or personal information, there is nothing for us to retain. Your scanned PDFs persist on your device until you delete them inside the app or uninstall ScanFast.

Children's privacy

ScanFast is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to one of our third-party services, contact us at muhammadharisgift1@gmail.com and we will assist with any reasonable request.

Your choices

  • Withdraw ad-tracking consent: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > ScanFast.
  • Reset your AdMob advertising identifier: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising.
  • Delete all scanned data: uninstall ScanFast (all documents are removed with the app).

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected in the "Effective date" above. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
