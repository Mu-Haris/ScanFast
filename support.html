Frequently asked questions

Where are my scans saved?

Inside ScanFast's local app storage on your iPhone. They never leave your device. You can browse them anytime in the Docs tab and organize them into folders.

How do I delete a document?

Open the document and tap the trash icon, or use multi-select on Home (long-press a thumbnail, then tap Delete in the bottom toolbar).

How do I export to PDF?

Open any document, tap Share > Export, and pick a format (PDF or JPG). You can email, save to Files, or send to any installed share extension.

Why am I seeing ads?

ScanFast is free and ad-supported. Ads keep development sustainable. They run only inside the document grid, viewer, and Settings — never during scanning or editing.

How do I turn off personalized ads?

Open iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > ScanFast and turn the toggle off. Ads will still appear, but won't be personalized using your advertising identifier.

The camera button isn't working.

Make sure ScanFast has camera permission: iOS Settings > ScanFast > Camera. If it's already enabled and the issue persists, try closing and re-opening the app.

Can I edit a page after saving?

Yes. Open the document, tap a page thumbnail, then Edit. You can re-crop, re-enhance, or replace the page.

How do I move documents between folders?

Long-press a document thumbnail to enter multi-select, pick the documents you want, then tap Move in the bottom toolbar.
